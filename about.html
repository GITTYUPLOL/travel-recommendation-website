About TravelMate

TravelMate is a travel-recommendation website built to help curious travelers discover beaches, temples, and countries worth visiting. Our team curates a small but high-signal catalog of destinations across Asia, the Pacific, and the Americas — with a focus on the kind of places that travelers tell their friends about for years.

Our mission

To make trip planning fast and inspiring. Most search engines return everything; we return the things you'll actually remember.

How to use the site

  • Browse — Click Home to see all destinations.
  • Search — Type a category keyword (beach, temple, or country) into the search bar and click Search.
  • Reset — Click Clear to reset the results to the full catalog.
  • Suggest a destination — Visit Contact Us and send us a note.

For team members

If you're contributing to the catalog, edit script.js and add a new object to the destinations array with the fields name, type, image, and description. Keep descriptions under two sentences and pick images that load over HTTPS.
